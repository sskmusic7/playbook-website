Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how COTMM ("Cut Out The Middle Man") ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our tools and dashboards connected to platforms such as Instagram, Facebook and Threads.

By using COTMM, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

1. Who we are

COTMM is a tool used to analyse and manage social media content and performance for creators and businesses.

Contact email: sskmusic7@gmail.com

2. Information we collect

When you connect your social media accounts (for example via Facebook Login / Instagram Graph API), we may receive:

  • Account information – name, username, profile picture, page name and IDs.
  • Content & insights – posts, captions, comments, media metadata, engagement metrics and other analytics that the platform's API makes available.
  • Technical information – access tokens and related authentication data, basic usage logs such as request time, browser type and IP address (for security and debugging).

We do not receive your social media passwords.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the COTMM dashboard and related services
  • Fetch and analyse your posts and insights to generate reports, suggestions and automation
  • Maintain and secure the service (debugging, monitoring, preventing abuse)
  • Communicate with you about updates, issues or support requests

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Legal basis (for UK / EU users)

Where applicable (e.g. under UK GDPR), we process your data based on:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide the service you requested.
  • Legitimate interests – to maintain and improve our tools and protect against misuse.
  • Consent – when you explicitly connect your social accounts or opt in to specific features.

You can revoke access at any time by disconnecting COTMM's permissions in your Facebook / Instagram settings.

5. Sharing your information

We may share your data with:

  • Service providers that help us host, store or process data (e.g. cloud hosting, databases, analytics).
  • Platform providers (Meta / Facebook / Instagram / Threads) as required by their APIs and policies.

We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or the rights and safety of others.

6. Data retention

We keep your data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

You can ask us to delete your data or disconnect your accounts at any time (see "Your rights" below).

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at sskmusic7@gmail.com.
