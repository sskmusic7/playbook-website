Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 3 December 2025

This page explains how to request deletion of your data from Playbook MPR and COTMM ("Cut Out The Middle Man") ("we", "us", "our").

How to Request Data Deletion

If you want us to delete your COTMM data, please contact us with your account details at one of the following email addresses:

Email us at:

  • kayah.mitchell@playbookmpr.com

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your name or username
  • The email address associated with your account
  • The social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, Threads) that were connected
  • A clear statement that you want your data deleted

Revoking Access via Facebook/Instagram Settings

You can also revoke COTMM's access to your social media accounts directly through your platform settings:

For Facebook/Instagram:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites" or "Business Integrations"
  3. Find "COTMM" or "Playbook MPR" in the list
  4. Click "Remove" or "Revoke Access"

Note: Revoking access through Facebook/Instagram settings will immediately stop COTMM from accessing your account data. However, if you want us to delete any data we have already collected and stored, you should also send us an email request as described above.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your account information (name, username, profile picture, page name, IDs)
  • Content and insights data (posts, captions, comments, media metadata, engagement metrics)
  • Access tokens and authentication data
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
